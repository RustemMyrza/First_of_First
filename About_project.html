About our project

Our project is anonymous chat. We made this chat especially for people who are afraid of society, or have some psychological trauma in this regard, and of course for introverts. The meaning of our project is that you can chat with any people regardless of their appearance, character, etc. all this was implemented so that there is no avatar where you can upload photos, you will not see nicknames, you will not be able to send voice messages, only chat. This is just a dream come true for introverts. and we made this site especially for them

The project was made by Myrzagaliyev Rustem and Zhusipnazar Alikhan especially for the IITU
